About

Fine artist based in Stroud, working with photography and film. I did my undergraduate degree at UAL in Graphic Design and worked for many years designing websites for charities before doing an MA in Fine Art at Bath Spa. Working with photography and film, I am currently exploring narratives about women. My practice is socially engaged and aims to encourage collective participation. I am interested in how invisible forces have power over our lives through images, cultural practices, and language; and within this context, how social change happens.
